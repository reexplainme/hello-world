I've built a social app that got me through a Google interview!

When I started, I was rather skeptical about the service, but when I met Jane (my coach), my world had changed. Since then I improved my coding skills tenfold, built up a nice portfolio on GitHub, and even put out my own app that matches people based on their favorite lasagna places in SF.

Frankly speaking, I don't think I would be able to achieve the same results if I taught myself or if I was working on something I knew would never be used. On the other hand, you guys made it rather interesting by mixing theory and practice in healthy amounts. My coach Jane have replaced StackOverlow for me and boy I'm so happy about that!
